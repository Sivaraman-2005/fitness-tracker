Strength Training Workout Plan

Build muscle and strength with this structured workout plan!

Warm-Up (5-10 minutes)

  • Dynamic stretches (leg swings, arm circles) – 2 minutes
  • Bodyweight squats – 2 minutes
  • Jumping jacks or brisk walking – 2 minutes

Main Workout (40-60 minutes)

  • Squats (or Goblet Squats) – 4 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Push-ups – 4 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Rows – 4 sets of 8-12 reps per arm
  • Deadlifts – 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Lunges (or Step-Ups) – 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  • Plank – 3 sets, hold for 30-60 seconds
  • Overhead Press – 3 sets of 8-12 reps

Cool-Down (5-10 minutes)

  • Light jogging or walking – 2 minutes
  • Static stretching (focus on legs, back, shoulders) – 3-5 minutes

Stay strong and train smart!
