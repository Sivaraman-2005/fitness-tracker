Cardio Workout Plan

Get your heart pumping and boost your fitness with this cardio workout!

Warm-Up (5-10 minutes)

  • Jog in place – 2 minutes
  • Jumping jacks – 2 minutes
  • Dynamic stretches – 1-2 minutes

Main Workout (20-30 minutes)

  • High knees – 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Mountain climbers – 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Burpees – 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Jump rope (or pretend) – 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Running in place – 3 sets of 1 minute

Cool-Down (5-10 minutes)

  • Light jogging or walking – 2 minutes
  • Static stretching – 3-5 minutes

Stay active and healthy!
