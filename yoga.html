Yoga Workout Plan

Relax, stretch, and strengthen your body with this peaceful yoga routine.

Warm-Up (5-10 minutes)

  • Cat-Cow Stretch – 1 minute
  • Child's Pose – 1 minute
  • Downward Dog – 1 minute
  • Standing Forward Bend – 1 minute

Main Poses (20-30 minutes)

  • Mountain Pose (Tadasana) – 2 minutes
  • Warrior I (Virabhadrasana I) – 2 minutes per side
  • Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II) – 2 minutes per side
  • Tree Pose (Vrksasana) – 2 minutes per side
  • Downward Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana) – 3 minutes
  • Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana) – 2 minutes
  • Bridge Pose (Setu Bandhasana) – 2 minutes
  • Seated Forward Bend (Paschimottanasana) – 3 minutes

Cool-Down (5-10 minutes)

  • Seated Twist (Ardha Matsyendrasana) – 2 minutes per side
  • Legs Up the Wall Pose (Viparita Karani) – 5 minutes
  • Corpse Pose (Savasana) – 5 minutes

Find your inner peace and strength!
